## Summary (from the public teaser)

The guide's premise: a dedicated **AI product sense** round has become standard at top AI companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind), was explicitly added to loops at Meta and Figma, and is woven into traditional rounds elsewhere. The bar has risen — generic product-sense frameworks like CIRCLES don't solve AI-specific questions — and this round increasingly determines a candidate's *level* and compensation rather than being a simple pass/fail.

The public portion centers on a worked mock-interview question described as one of the hardest currently circulating: **"How would you increase Claude Code weekly active users 10x?"** It also features a guest (a former Meta Group PM) noting that while ~70–80% of rounds remain traditional, every top AI company in his search included an AI product sense component. The detailed thinking shifts, scoring, and per-company breakdowns are paywalled.
